# Uber-Revenue-Analysis

## Project Overview
This project explores a dataset of 2,000 Uber users to identify why the top 25 highest-earning drivers are exclusively concentrated in Houston and Los Angeles. 

## Key Insights
- **Market Dominance:** Houston and Los Angeles lead in both total revenue and rider population.
- **The LA Supply Gap:** Despite having the **lowest number of active drivers (78)**, Los Angeles drivers are among the top earners. This indicates high demand-per-driver, likely leading to more frequent trips and higher individual revenue compared to New York (98 drivers).
- **The Success Formula:** High rider volume combined with optimized driver-to-rider ratios makes Houston and LA the most profitable markets in this dataset.
